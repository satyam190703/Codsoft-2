Fuel Economy and Real-World MPG

The Civic Type R gets an EPA-estimated 22 mpg in the city and 28 mpg on the
highway.While that matches the all-wheel-drive Golf R's EPA rating when optioned
with a six-speed manual,the Golf R leads by 2 mpg on the highway when fitted with
the eight-speed dual-clutch automatic.During our 75-mph highway fuel-economy
route, the Civic Type R beat its EPA estimate with 30 mpg.
